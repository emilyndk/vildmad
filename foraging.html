What is foraging?

Foraging is a method of searching for food in natural environments such as forests, fields, and shorelines. It involves gathering wild plants, fruits, nuts, and sometimes even insects for consumption. Foragers explore their surroundings, identifying edible items and harvesting them for sustenance. .
